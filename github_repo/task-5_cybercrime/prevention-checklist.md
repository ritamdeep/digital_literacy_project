# **Stay Safe Online – Checklist**

## **✅ Do’s**

* Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).  
* Keep your devices and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.  
* Verify links, emails, and messages before clicking—especially from unknown sources.  
* Use secure Wi-Fi networks; avoid logging into important accounts on public Wi-Fi.  
* Adjust privacy settings on social media to control who can see your information.  
* Regularly back up important data to prevent loss in case of cyberattacks.

## **💰 Financial / UPI Safety**

* Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank.  
* Always check whether you are **paying** or **receiving** money—UPI apps require a PIN only to send money.  
* Avoid scanning unknown QR codes or accepting payment requests from strangers.

## **❌ Don’ts**

* Don’t download apps or files from untrusted websites or links.  
* Don’t overshare personal information like address, phone number, or college ID online.  
* Don’t ignore suspicious account activity—act immediately.

## **🚨 Report Cybercrime**

* Report incidents at the **National Cyber Crime Portal:** cybercrime.gov.in  
* Call the **Cyber Crime Helpline:** 1930
