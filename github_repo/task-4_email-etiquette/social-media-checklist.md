# Social Media Checklist for Responsible Use

## ✅ Do’s  
- Do think before posting any content online.  
- Do protect your privacy by adjusting account settings.  
- Do verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news.  
- Do maintain respectful and polite communication.  
- Do use social media for learning, networking, and skill development.

## ❌ Don’ts  
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly.  
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying or negative comments.  
- Don’t believe or spread unverified news or rumors.  
- Don’t waste excessive time scrolling aimlessly.  
- Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content that may harm your reputation.
